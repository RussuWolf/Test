Who
we are?

Urban Development Group (UDG) - property investment, development and managment company focusing on residental and comercial property development in CEE countries. Busines was founded as a number of privately-held companies and investments mostly in residential property in 2008 and structured into group of companies with same investors in 2015. UDG boasts over USD 40 million worth of property developments in its up to five year development pipeline. The group has currently 7 ha of land under development for residential and comercial purposes. Operating as an integrated property development provider, UDG business activities cover land identification and acquistion, planning, financing, resourse management, project marceting, building design and construction of residential.
